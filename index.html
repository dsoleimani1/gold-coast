What We Do

Agency Violations
We Resolve

We work across all major NYC regulatory agencies to address violations, summonses, and compliance issues — from fire safety to building codes to sanitation.

FDNY
Fire Department of New York

Fire extinguisher violations, photoluminescent/egress lighting, fire suppression, missing permits, sprinkler systems, and all FDNY summonses and inspections.

DOB
Dept. of Buildings

Building permit violations, stop-work orders, illegal construction, certificate of occupancy issues, structural complaints, and DOB summonses.

HPD
Housing Preservation & Dev.

Housing maintenance violations, heat and hot water complaints, lead paint, rodent/pest issues, elevator violations, and HPD hazard class notices.

DSNY
Dept. of Sanitation

Trash placement, recycling non-compliance, littering summonses, containerization violations, and DSNY fines for commercial and residential properties.

EPA
Environmental Protection

Environmental violations, asbestos and hazardous materials, boiler compliance, air quality violations, and NYC DEP-related summonses.
